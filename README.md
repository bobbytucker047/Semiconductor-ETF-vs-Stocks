# Semiconductor-ETF-vs-Stocks
Showcasing benefits of ETF vs individual stock investing for long term growth.
I wrote this script to illustrate the benefits of choosing a diversified ETF, as opposed to playing a game of cat and mouse trying to select promising individual stocks. It is well known that companies like NVDA, AMD, and TSM have gigantic upside potential, but they also pose a big risk if any of those companies stumble. Even giants like Intel, as recent history demonstrates, can reach a peak and never return to previous values decades after.
